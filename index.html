UtilityBuild: A Tangible Philosophy

Discovery

We believe that technical and business decisions become one in the same when you build software. During Discovery we’ll research your market, validate proposed features, and write user stories.

Build Meetings

After Discovery, every 2 weeks are punctuated by a Build Meeting. Deliverables, budget and sales updates are examined. Design and branding are polished, and marketing strategies are put into place.

Beta Launch

Your Beta is the culmination of 8-12 weeks in the trenches, when you’ve hit the minimum Utility needed to launch, generate revenue and build your network. We’ll also utilize our own rolodex to arrange customers, partnerships, and a launch party.

Rinse & Repeat

After more user interviews and feedback, the release cycle continues. We’ve got your back as you sell what we’ve built and grow your business - again employing the UtilityBuild system for new features.
